Overview

What if I told you Aliens where real?

Well they are in fact real. However this is not something to celebrate. These extraterrestrial lifeform's goal is nothing of the total control of human kind

Among the community these are called the Donut Aliens or DAs for short. you might think it is rather a silly name but let me assure you that this is most likely the destruction of a free human kind unless we do something about it. These aliens brought donuts to earth. You may think that this is a good thing but you could not be more wrong. Every donut a person eats more of their mind is taken over by the DAs. After a certain amount of donuts the DAs will completely take over your mind.

Apperence

These lifeforms look very similar to their mind controlling creation. (see artist recreation to the right) Their outer skin is most often perfectly smooth and glossy, but some mutations they get cause the texture of their skin to change. One variant causes the skin to begin to break down into tiny little white particles, this was the inspiration for the powdered donut. The skin can also wrinkle up which is where the inspiration for the cake donut. one other mutation is they get many colorful warts popping up, you can probably guess what this inspired, sprinkles of coarse.They most often appear Torus, but when they eat the hole in the middle closes around their current meal. When fully digested it turns into a cream, jam, or chocolate like substance. The Donut aliens then survive off of this filling, similar to how earth animals use fat. soon after eating a small hole opens up and expands as the creature uses its stored energy until eventually growing back to its normal size meaning it is ready to eat again.
